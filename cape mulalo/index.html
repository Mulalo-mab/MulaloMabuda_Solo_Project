Visit Cape Town

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district close to the ocean.

Top three activities to do at Cape Town

Table Mountain Aerial Cableway

Rotating floor cable car ride with panoramic 360-degree city and mountain views.

Bo-kaap

Colourful historic neighborhood with a relaxed ambiance, vibrant streets ideal for photography, and culture significance.

Two Oceans Aquarium

Aquarium sanctuary at V&A Waterfront displaying ecosystems and marine life including penguins and sharks.
